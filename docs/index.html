About Me

I am a PhD student at University of California, Riverside, advised by Prof. Heng Yin. Before joining UCR, I earned my B.E. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology. My research interests lie in the field of computer security, where I specialize in developing advanced program analysis techniques (including concolic execution, program fuzz testing, and hybrid fuzzing) for uncovering vulnerabilities within systems and software. More details about me can be found in my CV.
